❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start mining?

Go to the dashboard and tap the Mine button. Mining starts automatically for 24 hours.

How do I earn TrustScore?

You gain TrustScore by mining daily, inviting friends, and uploading studio content.

Is KYC required?

Not yet. Mainnet-KYC will launch soon. Stay tuned via announcements.

What if I forget my login?

Use the "Forgot Password" on login screen or contact support via Telegram.

How can I change my phone number?

Email a request to Santorumsantus@gmail.com to begin phone update process.
